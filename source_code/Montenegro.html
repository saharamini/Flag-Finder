Montenegro - Country in the Balkans

Montenegro is a Balkan country with rugged mountains, medieval villages and a narrow strip of beaches along its Adriatic coastline. The Bay of Kotor, resembling a fjord, is dotted with coastal churches and fortified towns such as Kotor and Herceg Novi. Durmitor National Park, home to bears and wolves, encompasses limestone peaks, glacial lakes and 1,300m-deep Tara River Canyon. Wikipedia
Capital: Podgorica
Currency: Euro
Prime minister: Milo Đukanović
Official script: Montenegrin
