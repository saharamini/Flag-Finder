Togo - Country in West Africa

Togo, a West African nation on the Gulf of Guinea, is known for its palm-lined beaches and hilltop villages. Koutammakou, inhabited by the Batammariba people, is a traditional settlement of fortresslike clay huts dating to the 17th century. In the capital, Lomé, are the multistory Grand Marché bazaar and the Fetish Market, offering traditional talismans and remedies relating to the vodun (voodoo) religion. Wikipedia
Capital: Lomé
Currency: CFA franc
President:Faure Gnassingbé
Official script: French
Population: 7.606 million (2016) World Bank
