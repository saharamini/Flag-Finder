Lithuania - Country in Europe

Lithuania is a country and the southernmost of Europe’s Baltic states, a former Soviet bloc nation bordering Poland, Latvia and Belarus. Its capital, Vilnius, near the Belarus border, is known for its medieval Old Town. It also has Gothic, Renaissance and baroque architecture, and 18th-century cathedral built on a pagan temple site. Hilltop Gediminas' Tower, a symbol of the city and the nation, offers sweeping views. Wikipedia
Capital: Vilnius
Currency:Euro
President: Dalia Grybauskaitė
Population: 2.872 million (2016) World Bank
